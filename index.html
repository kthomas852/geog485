Welcome to Better Bike ABQ

So, where are the safest roads to ride a bike in Albuquerque?
As a bicycle enthusiast it was my personal goal to answer this question. It started as
a class project and grew into this website. What I discovered were the many areas of town
that bost a high incident of accidents. The factors for these accidents are many, as it
is difficult to boil down the cause to just one factor. This site hopes to highlight Danger
Zones in the ABQ metro area as well as to provide some sage advice for best practices for riding
in the city. It is my hope to improve ridership as a whole and to keep both riders and drivers safe.
Enjoy!
