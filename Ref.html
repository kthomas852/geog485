Rules of the road:
Always wear a helmet
Before riding out of a driveway into the street, stop. Look left, then right, and then left again for traffic
Ride on the right side of the street, with the flow of traffic
Stop at all stop signs and look left, right, and left again for traffic
Use reflectors and bicycle lights as they are required for night riding

Best Riding Practices:
Remember when riding that you are considered a vehicle by law. Stay off the sidewalk. Drivers
are less likely to see you on the sidewalk and will be running you over before they ever realize
you are there. As a cyclist you are most visible in the middle of the far right lane or in a
designated bicycle lane. Look drivers in the eye when approaching a turning car and never be
afraid to give them a "friendly" wave. This will help them see you and help brighten their day
and yours. When
